cortex.volume.mosaic¶

cortex.volume.mosaic(data, dim=0, show=True, **kwargs)[source]¶

Turns volume data into a mosaic, useful for quickly viewing volumetric data with radiological convention (left side of figure is right side of subject).

Parameters
dataarray_like

3D volumetric data to mosaic

dimint

Dimension across which to mosaic. Default 0.

showbool

Display mosaic with matplotlib? Default True.
